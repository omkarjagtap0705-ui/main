Information of Shardul Thakur


About

Shardul Thakur is an Indian international all-rounder known for his "partnership-breaking" bowling and aggressive lower-order batting. He represents the Mumbai Indians in the IPL after a trade from the Lucknow Super Giants.

In his IPL career, he has played 114 matches and taken 119 wickets, winning titles with Chennai Super Kings in 2018 and 2021.
