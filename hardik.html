Information of Hardik Pandya


About

Hardik Pandya is an explosive all-rounder who plays as a right-handed batter and right-arm medium-fast bowler.

Originally scouted and nurtured by the Mumbai Indians (MI) in 2015 for just ₹10 Lakhs, he evolved into a premier finisher, playing a pivotal role in their title victories

IPL Career & Teams

Mumbai Indians (2015–2021): Established himself as a vital cog in the middle order and death bowling, helping the franchise secure four IPL titles between 2015 and 2020.

Gujarat Titans (2022–2023): Drafted as the captain of the Ahmedabad-based franchise. He led the team to their maiden IPL title in their debut year in 2022 and a runner-up finish in 2023.

Mumbai Indians (2024–Present): Returned to MI in a blockbuster homecoming trade ahead of the 2024 season, taking over the captaincy.


Career Highlights & Records

Breakthrough Season: His most prolific all-round season came in 2019, where he hammered 402 runs at a staggering strike rate of 191.41 and claimed 14 wickets.

Capacitance Success: After leading GT to the 2022 title, he became the first captain since Shane Warne (2008) to win the championship in his team's debut season.

Trading History: His transfer back to MI ahead of 2024 involved a reported ₹100 Crore all-cash deal. Under his leadership, MI qualified for the Playoffs in 2025.
