Information of Rohit Sharma


About

Rohit Sharma is the joint most successful captain in Indian Premier League (IPL) history, having led the Mumbai Indians to five championship titles (2013, 2015, 2017, 2019, and 2020).

A premier batter and former captain, he has scored over 7,000 runs in the tournament and holds the record for the most sixes by an Indian in the competition.

Here is an overview of his legendary IPL career:


Career Highlights & Records

Teams Represented: Deccan Chargers (2008–2010), Mumbai Indians (2011–Present).

Titles Won: 6 (1 with Deccan Chargers, 5 with Mumbai Indians).

Career Runs: Amassed over 7,000 runs, making him one of the top run-scorers in the history of the league.

Centuries: 2 centuries, including a top score of 105 not out against the Chennai Super Kings.


Batting Milestones

Sixes: Became the first Indian to smash 300 sixes in IPL history.

Longevity: He entered his 19th consecutive IPL edition in 2026.

Captaincy: After being acquired by MI in 2011, he took over the captaincy in 2013 and led the franchise for 11 years until being succeeded by Hardik Pandya in 2024.
