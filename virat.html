Information of Virat Kohali


About

Virat Kohli is the all-time leading run-scorer in Indian Premier League (IPL) history, playing exclusively for the Royal Challengers Bengaluru (RCB) since 2008. He holds the league records for the most runs in a single season (973 runs in 2016) and the most career centuries

Kohli has established himself as the most formidable and consistent batter in the tournament's history. Here are his primary IPL career highlights and records:


Career Runs : Virat Kohli Profile has amassed over 8,600 runs in more than 260+ matches.

Single-Season Record : During the 2016 season, he scored a staggering 973 runs with 4 centuries—an unbreakable single-season record.

Centuries : He has scored 9 centuries in the IPL, placing him at the top of the all-time list.

Orange Cap : He won the Orange Cap (award for the leading run-scorer in a season) in both 2016 and 2024.

Captaincy : He served as the full-time captain of RCB from 2013 to 2021.

Championship : Kohli won his maiden IPL title in the 2025 season, where RCB defeated the Punjab Kings in the final.
